Frequently Asked Questions

Common support questions

Quick answers for the most common requests and support issues.

How do I book an appointment?

Choose “Book Appointment” in the form, enter your details, and submit your preferred date and time.

How do I reschedule a booking?

Select “Reschedule Appointment” in the form and provide your new preferred time and date.

How can I submit a complaint?

Select “Complaint” in the request type field and describe the issue clearly in the message box.

How long does support take?

Support requests are reviewed as quickly as possible during working hours.
